About:

Formed by school friends in 2005, M.A.D have been peddling their unique, new brand of Thrash Metal throughout the UK for over 10 years. Having played with the likes of Destruction, Onslaught and Gama Bomb, M.A.D have continued to evolve; introducing more left-field and crossover influences into their sound, making for a completely unique auditory experience!

M.A.D have not only become popular in brighton recently, But are heading off around the UK on their Debut tour in june/july this year playing at various venues and pubs. They'll be ending the tour back in brighton their home town of origin, Also the band have started to branch into playing special events such as weddings,birthdays and general gigs. So if you are looking for a Thrash metal band that not only writes their own material, can play any cover song and have high availability to perform. Then go to the book-ins page, to see availability and book us for your event today!
